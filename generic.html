Over the past few years Luis has developed the technical skills needed for career in Data Science, including a competitive Master's degree in Data Science. Every data science project taught him new skills. Check out Luis's portfolio featuring a compilation of data science projects.

Luis Valenzuela

Data Science and Data Analytics professional with 4 years of professional experience. Expert in subject knowledge of Business Intelligence, Predictive Analytics, Statistical Modeling, and Process Optimization and NLP. Data science projects include: Hate Speech Detection using NLP, Credit Card Fraud Detection, Lung Cancer Prediction, and Twitter Text Sentiment Analysis to name a few.

Professional experience varies from different industries that include Life Sciences, Finance, and Technology. In 2012 Luis double majored in International Business and Economics from California State University of Los Angeles. Young and motivated he worked a lot of odd jobs and moved his way up the corporate ladder through hard work and determination. Eventually setting his sights for the big apple. Luis moved to New York City in 2012. After many years working in data analytic roles he felt inspired to continue his education with a STEM degree. Luis wanted to build on his data analytics background and work toward his dream of being a Data Scientist.

In May of 2023 Luis graduated with distinctions from NYIT with a Master of Science in Data Science.
